The Hidden Pairs method is similar to the nakedPairs method. To use the Hidden Pairs method, begin by choosing a box, row, or column in the sudoku. If there are two missing numbers in the box/row/column that could only be put in the same two cells, any other candidates that could go in those two cells can be eliminated, because those cells must contain the two numbers that could only go in those cells.
